What is Anger Management?

- Anger management is a process for identifying the triggers of anger and learning strategies to handle it in a constructive way. Effective anger management can help individuals avoid conflicts and improve relationships.

- It involves understanding your anger, recognizing the signs before it escalates, and practicing techniques to manage your responses. This can lead to better emotional control and more positive interactions.

- Anger management is how we handle situations that make us angry. It's also a form of cognitive behavioral therapy (sometimes called talk therapy) that can take place in one-on-one or group counseling.
